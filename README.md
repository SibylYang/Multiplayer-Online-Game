# Multiplayer-Online-Game
This game is called Clueless and allows multiple players to play up to six characters at the same time. Players can choose how many people will play and choose to move, suggest, or accuse along the game. The winner is the one who accuse the correct character, weapon, and room.

### Menu Screen
Players are able to choose the number of players and characters at the begining of the game.

### Game Playing Screen
On the right of the screen, players will be able to see a notification panel, three columns of cards, and options panel. Notification panel shows the status of the game such as whose turn and what other players have done. The three columns of cards will be bolded once clicked so players can track what they know. The options panel show what actions can the player take.
